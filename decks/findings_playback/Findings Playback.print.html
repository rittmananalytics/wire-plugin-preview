06

Introduction

Analytics Hierarchy of Needs

  • Like Maslow's hierarchy of needs, analytics needs can be organised into a hierarchy
  • Having clean data relies on it being effectively collected
  • Defining and tracking data relies on it being collected and clean
  • Analysing data well relies on it being defined properly, etc.
  • We classify each requirement to the lowest tier whose absence is blocking it

07

Introduction

People, Process & Technology (PPT)

  • Established framework recognising that people, process and technology together drive action
  • Scale, Innovation and Automation each require combinations of two of the three
  • Organisations that win have all three in harmony
  • We classify each requirement to one axis — the one whose absence is causing it
