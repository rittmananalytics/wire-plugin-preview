Our methodology

People, process, technology — in that order.

1

People

We work alongside your team — not in a back room. Pair programming and knowledge transfer are non-negotiable from day one.

  • Senior consultants only — no juniors, no rotation
  • Pair programming with your data engineers
  • Advisory partnership beyond go-live
2

Process

Two-week sprints with planning, demo and retro. You always know where we are, and the work compounds visibly.

  • Two-week sprint cadence
  • Weekly demos & retrospectives
  • Single shared Jira board, full client access
3

Technology

An opinionated, modern stack we know inside out. Looker, dbt, BigQuery — Google Cloud Data Analytics Specialised Partner.

  • Looker semantic layer + BigQuery
  • dbt / Dataform for transformations
  • Version-controlled, documented, scalable

Target architecture
